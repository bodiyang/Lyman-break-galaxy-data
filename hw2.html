##The Euclidean method I’m using the Euclidean method to complete this study, by following the instruction post by the instructiors. Briefly, the x-coordinates of wavelenth is thought to be irrelevant because of redshift, and we will compare the similiarity of cB_58 and each spectrum based on the Euclidean distances of y-coordinates of flux. The spectrum with smallest Euclidean distance will the ones we are looking for.

To be noticed, y-coordinates will be substracted the mean value of elements in each vector and divided by the standard deviation of elements in each vector. This is to align specta of different brightness and distances.
